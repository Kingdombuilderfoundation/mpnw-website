About MPNW

More Than a Pageant.
A Movement.

Founded in 2025 and now proudly in our second year, Miss Philippines Northwest USA is a leadership and empowerment platform for Filipina women, supported by the Kingdom Builder Foundation — a federally recognized 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.

We develop women through culture, confidence, community service, professional growth, and mentorship — creating opportunities that extend far beyond the competition stage.

Our platform represents the Pacific Northwest and connects candidates to national and international stages, including a direct pathway to Miss Universe Philippines.

"To empower Filipina women across the Pacific Northwest by celebrating culture, confidence, leadership, and community service while developing them as leaders on and off the stage."

What is Aces & Queens?

Aces & Queens is the Philippines' most prestigious pageant training camp, founded in 2001. Credited for transforming the Philippines into a global beauty pageant powerhouse, their coaches have trained Miss Universe, Miss World, and Miss International titleholders. According to Agence France Presse, their methods helped make the Philippines "a beauty pageant superpower."

For the first time, Aces & Queens is bringing their world-class coaching team to the United States in partnership with MPNW — and our queens Jacqueline Aluning, Rachel Hanna Gozum, and Jayka Noelle Munsayac will be joining as featured guests.

The two-day intensive covers six signature training modules:

01Personality Development
02Image & Styling
03Hair & Makeup
04Q&A Preparation
05Pasarela (Pageant Walk)
06Fitness & Stage Presence

Open to everyone — no pageant experience required. Whether you are an aspiring queen, a titleholder, or simply want to build confidence and stage presence, this boot camp is for you.
